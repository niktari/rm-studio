R&M is a graphic design studio located in Bushwick, Brooklyn, specializing in identity systems and typography. Officially established in 2022, it is led by Ryan Bugden and Michelle Ando, both with years of experience at top NYC design shops.

The studio caters to small businesses across diverse sectors — food & drink, fashion, arts & culture, music, lifestyle, non-profit, and wellness — aiming to craft unique visual identities that align with each client’s goals.
